City of Syracuse Performance Dashboard

The City of Syracuse has a mission to consistently deliver high-quality experiences through proactive and efficient service. To do that, we must start by being transparent about our services and pursue data-driven results. Our performance dashboard communicates our city objectives and measures our progress toward those objectives from start to achievement. We are sharing specific and measureable goals that will be updated daily and reviewed weekly with leaders on our team.

Objective: Achieve Fiscal Sustainability

The City is on a path toward achieving fiscal sustainability. To show our city's growth, we'll have to show that we can manage spending and grow revenue. The City projected an $11 million budget deficit for the 2019 Fiscal Year (July 2018-June 2019). The key results for Achieving Fiscal Sustainability will both allow for and be a signal of a growing city.

Increase economic investment and neighborhood stability

The City of Syracuse should be a place where it is easy to open a business and live in quality, safe housing. We are taking several measures to improve how we attract economic investments to our city and stabilize our neighborhoods.

Objective: Provide quality constituent engagement and response

The community is an important part of how we govern. To ensure the community can interact with city government easily, we are identifying and improving ways that the community voice should be heard early and frequently. City government will work to provide quality constituent engagement and response to improve our public engagement strategies.

Objective: Deliver city services effectively, efficiently, and equitably

The City delivers several services that require coordination, inter-departmental communication, and high-quality delivery. The City of Syracuse should reliably deliver services, like filling potholes, in a reasonable time period, and do the work completely, so that revisits for the same problem occur rarely.
